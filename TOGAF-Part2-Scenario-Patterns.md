# TOGAF® 10 – Part 2 Scenario Question Patterns  
*The 12 recurring patterns used in official TOGAF scenario questions*

These patterns appear repeatedly in the official Question Book and real exam.  
Recognising them dramatically improves your ability to select the 5‑point answer.

---

# ⭐ Pattern 1 — “What Should the Architect Do Next?”
The most common pattern.  
Correct answer = **next ADM step**, not a leap ahead.

---

# ⭐ Pattern 2 — “Which ADM Phase Applies?”
Look for:
- keywords  
- activities  
- deliverables  
- stakeholder behaviours  

Correct answer = **phase whose objectives match the scenario**.

---

# ⭐ Pattern 3 — “Which Deliverable or Artifact Is Needed?”
Often tests:
- Architecture Definition Document  
- Architecture Requirements Specification  
- Roadmap  
- Gap Analysis  

Correct answer = **deliverable produced in the current phase**.

---

# ⭐ Pattern 4 — “Stakeholder Conflict”
Correct answer:
- engage stakeholders  
- clarify concerns  
- update stakeholder map  
- communicate architecture intent  

Never choose “ignore”, “escalate immediately”, or “proceed without alignment”.

---

# ⭐ Pattern 5 — “Requirements Change”
Correct answer:
- update the **Requirements Repository**  
- follow **Requirements Management**  
- assess impact on ADM phases  

Never jump straight to implementation changes.

---

# ⭐ Pattern 6 — “Gap Analysis Interpretation”
Correct answer:
- identify gaps  
- classify gaps  
- feed into Opportunities & Solutions  

Avoid answers that:
- propose solutions  
- design systems  
- implement technology  

---

# ⭐ Pattern 7 — “Migration Planning”
Correct answer:
- prioritise work packages  
- create transition architectures  
- update the roadmap  

Avoid:
- detailed project planning  
- solution delivery tasks  

---

# ⭐ Pattern 8 — “Governance vs. Delivery”
Correct answer = **architecture governance**, not solution delivery.

Incorrect answers often describe:
- coding  
- configuring systems  
- building solutions  
- running projects  

---

# ⭐ Pattern 9 — “Architecture Vision Clarity”
Correct answer:
- refine scope  
- identify stakeholders  
- confirm constraints  
- develop the Vision  

Avoid jumping into detailed architecture work.

---

# ⭐ Pattern 10 — “Risk or Issue Identified”
Correct answer:
- update risk log  
- assess impact  
- engage stakeholders  
- adjust architecture work  

Avoid:
- ignoring the risk  
- escalating prematurely  
- implementing fixes directly  

---

# ⭐ Pattern 11 — “Change Request”
Correct answer:
- evaluate the change  
- determine ADM impact  
- follow Phase H or Requirements Management  

Avoid:
- implementing the change immediately  
- bypassing governance  

---

# ⭐ Pattern 12 — “Architecture Governance”
Correct answer:
- conduct compliance reviews  
- ensure alignment with principles  
- escalate only when necessary  

Avoid:
- bypassing governance  
- approving solutions without review  

---

# 📌 Summary
These 12 patterns cover **90%+** of TOGAF Part 2 scenario logic.  
If you can recognise the pattern, you can identify the 5‑point answer.

Use this cheat‑sheet alongside the **Part 2 Reasoning Framework** for maximum exam performance.
